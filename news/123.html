IGC 2020 at Delhi, India, 2-8 March 2020

The 36th International Geological Congress (IGC 2020 or 36IGC) will be organized in Delhi, India on 2-8 March, 2020.

The congress website is http://www.36igc.org/.

The first circular is now available. The pdf file is about 17.8MB. You can download it from the congress website or the ICS website.
