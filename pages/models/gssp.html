Global Standard Stratigraphic Age c

IRI http://resource.geosciml.org/ontology/gssp/GSSA
Is Defined By GSSP Model
Description

GSSAs are boundaries of intervals in geologic time defined by by assigning them a specific numerical age (usually in millions of years ago)

Global Boundary Stratotype Sections and Point c

IRI http://resource.geosciml.org/ontology/gssp/GSSP
Is Defined By Stratigraphy Model
Description

GSSPs are reference points on stratigraphic sections of rock which define the lower boundaries of stages on the International Chronostratigraphic Chart
Equivalentclass gts:StratigraphicPoint
